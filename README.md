# Wholify 🌿

> **Change how you look at change.**

Wholify is a wellness challenge app built on a simple belief: discipline looks like showing up — not suffering through. A 30, 60, or 90-day challenge built around the 8 Elements of Wellness, with a grading system that gives you credit for every task you complete.

---

## What makes Wholify different

Most challenge apps are all-or-nothing. Miss one goal and the whole day is a failure. Wholify disagrees.

- **Grade-based scoring** — complete 4 of 5 tasks and you earn a B+. Effort is never wasted.
- **The W mark** — complete a day and earn your W. Your calendar becomes a visual grid of proof over time.
- **8 Wellness dimensions** — Physical, Emotional, Social, Intellectual, Spiritual, Environmental, Occupational, and Financial. You choose 3–6 to focus on.
- **3-day edit window** — life happens. You have 3 days to go back and log what you actually did.
- **Share-ready calendar** — one tap to share your W-mark grid to Instagram, X, or WhatsApp.
- **Year-end review** — at the end of the year, see everything you accomplished with fun comparisons ("You journaled enough to write Harry Potter twice").

## Core user flow

```
Splash → Onboarding → Challenge Setup → Calendar (daily W marks) → Results
```

1. **Onboarding** — name, goals (multi-select), notifications, avatar
2. **Challenge setup** — pick 30/60/90 days, select 3–6 wellness dimensions, choose tasks with custom targets
3. **Daily check-in** — tap tasks complete, see your live grade update, log your day
4. **Calendar** — W marks fill in over time, shareable as a social card
5. **Results** — grade, heat map by dimension, stat highlights
